  Welcome to Barrio Dose

We are a team of food enthusiasts who are passionate about showcasing the diverse and delicious flavors of Filipino cuisine. Our goal is to provide our customers with a taste of the authentic and traditional Filipino food that is cooked and served in carinderias all around the Philippines.

Carinderias are local eateries that serve affordable and tasty Filipino dishes that are cooked fresh daily. These small and humble establishments have been a part of Filipino culture for generations and are beloved by locals and tourists alike. Our team has spent countless hours researching and tasting different dishes from carinderias all over the Philippines. We carefully select our ingredients and prepare our dishes with the same care and attention to detail as the carinderias we feature.

We take pride in providing our customers with an authentic Filipino dining experience. From the aroma of our dishes to the warm hospitality of our staff, we strive to make our customers feel like they have been transported to the bustling streets of the Philippines. We hope you enjoy your visit to our Carinderia page and we look forward to sharing our love of Filipino cuisine with you.
